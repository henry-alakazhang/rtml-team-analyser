About .rtml

This is .rtml, a Pokemon team builder and analysis tool, by NinjaCaterpie.

Input your team to get an analysis of defensive typing and bulk, offensive typing and power and much more! (Currently in really really alpha).
http://github.com/henry-alakazhang/rtml-team-analyser
